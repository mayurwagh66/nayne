Who We Are

Sowing the Seeds of Empowerment and Change

Established in 2021 by a dynamic group of high school students during the challenging times of the pandemic, NayePankh Foundation started as a promise to safeguard the most vulnerable communities.

Our core philosophy is simple: give wings (Pankh) to every individual's inherent dreams and capabilities. Through constant direct action drives, transparency, and youth leadership, we convert compassion into measurable ground-level impact.

Our Core Missions

We work across dedicated thematic pillars to create holistic improvements in underprivileged lives.

Education

Project Shiksha

Empowering children through customized education modules, teaching camps, and regular supplies of notebooks, writing material, and uniforms.

Discover More
Hunger Relief

Project Annapurna

Combating child malnutrition and hunger by distributing nutritious meals, fresh fruits, and daily dry ration boxes to low-income clusters.

Discover More
Hygiene & Health

Project Nari

Breaking social taboos surrounding menstrual hygiene by hosting workshops, spreading medical awareness, and giving free biodegradable sanitary towels.
