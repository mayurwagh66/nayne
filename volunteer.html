Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about volunteering and internships.

Is there an age limit to join NayePankh?

No, we do not have strict age barriers. While we are a youth-led foundation run primarily by high school and college students, anyone with a passionate heart and a willingness to help underprivileged sections is welcome to apply.

Will I receive a certificate for my contribution?

Yes, all official volunteers and interns receive a formal certificate of appreciation and a letter of recommendation detailing their impact, hours served, and projects handled. These certificates are highly recognized in college applications and career paths.

Are on-ground drives safe?

Absolutely. We prioritize safety. All on-ground food distribution or medical camps are organized with local permissions, proper supervision, and protective hygiene wear. Student volunteers are always accompanied by lead project coordinators.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes. If you are not based in North India or cannot travel for field work, you can apply for our digital volunteer positions. These include graphic designing, content writing, social media management, translation services, and fundraising coordinate roles.
